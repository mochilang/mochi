# SPOJ Mixtures (MIXTURES)

https://www.spoj.com/problems/MIXTURES/

N mixtures in a row, each with a color (1, 2, or 3) and a quantity. Mixing two adjacent
groups produces smoke equal to (total quantity of left group) * (total quantity of right
group). The resulting mixture has the combined quantity and color = (sum of colors) % 3.
Find the minimum total smoke to merge all N mixtures into one.

## Approach

Interval DP:
- dp_smoke[i*N+j] = minimum smoke to merge mixtures i..j into one
- dp_qty[i*N+j] = total quantity of mixtures i..j (fixed, just sum)
- Base: dp_smoke[i*N+i] = 0, dp_qty[i*N+i] = qty[i]
- Transition: dp_smoke[i*N+j] = min over k in [i, j-1] of
    dp_smoke[i*N+k] + dp_smoke[(k+1)*N+j] + dp_qty[i*N+k] * dp_qty[(k+1)*N+j]

Iterate by increasing interval length.
