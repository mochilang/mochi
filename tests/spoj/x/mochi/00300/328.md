# SPOJ BISHOPS - Bishops

https://www.spoj.com/problems/BISHOPS/

Maximum number of non-attacking bishops on an n×n chessboard.
For n=1: answer is 1. For n>=2: answer is 2n-2.

## Approach

Bishops attack along diagonals. The two sets of diagonals (white and black
squares) are independent. For an n×n board, you can place at most 2n-2 bishops
(filling the two edge rows of diagonals). Special case n=1 gives 1.
