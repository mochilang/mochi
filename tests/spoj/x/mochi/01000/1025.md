# SPOJ Fashion Shows (FASHION)

https://www.spoj.com/problems/FASHION/

Given N models, each with a top score and a bottom score. Assign each model exactly
one top garment and one bottom garment (a permutation). Maximize the total score,
where the total is sum over all paired (top, bottom) of (top + bottom).

## Approach

Since total = sum(all tops) + sum(all bottoms) regardless of pairing, any permutation
gives the same total. The answer is simply the sum of all top scores plus all bottom scores.
