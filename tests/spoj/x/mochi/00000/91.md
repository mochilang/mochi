# SPOJ Two Squares or Not Two Squares (TWOSQRS)

https://www.spoj.com/problems/TWOSQRS/

Determine if integer N can be written as a sum of two squares.

## Approach

N = a^2 + b^2 iff in N's prime factorization, every prime p ≡ 3 (mod 4) appears
to an even power. Factorize N by trial division up to sqrt(N).
