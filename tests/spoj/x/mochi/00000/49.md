# SPOJ Hares and Foxes (HAREFOX)

https://www.spoj.com/problems/HAREFOX/

N hares and M foxes on a number line, all moving right. Hares move at speed H, foxes
at speed F. A fox catches a hare when they occupy the same position at time t >= 0.
Count the number of hares that survive.

## Approach

A fox at position fpos catches a hare at position hpos if:
- F > H and fpos <= hpos: the fox is faster and starts at or behind the hare (catches up), or
- F == H and fpos == hpos: same speed, same starting position (already at same place).

If F < H the hares are faster and always escape any fox behind them.

Input: each line has N M H F (number of hares, foxes, hare speed, fox speed), then a
line of N hare positions, then a line of M fox positions. EOF terminates input.
Output: number of surviving hares per test case.
