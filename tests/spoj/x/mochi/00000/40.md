# SPOJ Lifting the Stone (STONE)

https://www.spoj.com/problems/STONE/

Compute the centroid (center of mass) of a homogeneous polygon.

## Approach

For each edge (xi,yi)-(xi+1,yi+1), compute cross = xi*yi+1 - xi+1*yi.
- Area A = 0.5 * |sum(cross)|
- Cx = (1/(6*A)) * sum((xi+xi+1)*cross)
- Cy = (1/(6*A)) * sum((yi+yi+1)*cross)

Output Cx and Cy with 2 decimal places.
