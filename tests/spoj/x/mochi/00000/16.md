# SPOJ TETRA - Sphere in a Tetrahedron

https://www.spoj.com/problems/TETRA/

Given edge lengths a, b, c, d, e, f of a tetrahedron, find the radius of
the inscribed sphere. Output with 4 decimal places.

## Approach

Use the formula: r = 3 * V / A_total, where V is the volume and A_total is
the sum of areas of all four faces.

Volume via Cayley-Menger determinant, face areas via Heron's formula.

## Notes

Edge mapping: a=AB, b=AC, c=AD, d=BC, e=BD, f=CD.
