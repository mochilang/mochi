# SPOJ ONP - Transform the Expression

https://www.spoj.com/problems/ONP/

Convert an infix expression to postfix (RPN) notation.
Variables are single lowercase letters. Operators: +, -, *, /, ^.

## Approach

Shunting-yard algorithm: maintain an operator stack, output operands directly,
push operators and handle precedence/associativity, flush remaining operators at end.

## Notes

^ is right-associative (higher precedence). Parentheses are stripped.
