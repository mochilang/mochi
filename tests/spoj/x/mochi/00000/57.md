# SPOJ Supernumbers in a Permutation (SUPPER)

https://www.spoj.com/problems/SUPPER/

Given a permutation of 1..N, a number P[i] is a "supernumber" if it is greater
than all elements to its right. Find all supernumbers.

## Approach

Scan right to left tracking the running maximum. P[i] is super iff P[i] > current_max.
Collect in reverse, then output in original (left-to-right) order.
