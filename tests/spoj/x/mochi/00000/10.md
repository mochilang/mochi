# SPOJ CMEXPR - Complicated Expressions

https://www.spoj.com/problems/CMEXPR/

Given a fully parenthesized expression, remove all unnecessary parentheses
(those that do not change the meaning due to operator precedence/associativity).

## Approach

Parse the expression into an AST. Then recursively emit each node, adding
parentheses only when a child's precedence is lower than the parent's, or
when right-associativity would change the result.

## Notes

Rules: - is not commutative, / is not associative. Handle these as special cases
for the right child of - and the right child of /.
