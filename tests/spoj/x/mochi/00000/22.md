# SPOJ TRICENTR - Triangle From Centroid

https://www.spoj.com/problems/TRICENTR/

Given the centroid G(gx, gy) of a triangle and two vertices A(ax, ay) and B(bx, by),
find the third vertex C(cx, cy).

## Approach

The centroid is the average of the three vertices:
  gx = (Ax + Bx + Cx) / 3
  gy = (Ay + By + Cy) / 3

Solving for C:
  Cx = 3*gx - Ax - Bx
  Cy = 3*gy - Ay - By

Input: multiple lines, each with 6 integers: gx gy Ax Ay Bx By.
Output: Cx Cy for each line.
