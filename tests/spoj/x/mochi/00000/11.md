# SPOJ FCTRL - Factorial

https://www.spoj.com/problems/FCTRL/

Given n, count the number of trailing zeros in n!.

## Approach

Trailing zeros come from factors of 10 = 2 * 5. Since factors of 2 always outnumber
factors of 5, count how many times 5 divides into n!.

Count = floor(n/5) + floor(n/25) + floor(n/125) + ...

Each term counts multiples of 5, 25, 125, etc. up to n.
Works for n up to 10^9 (about 13 iterations before 5^k > n).
