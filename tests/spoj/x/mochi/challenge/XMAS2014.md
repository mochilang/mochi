# XMAS2014 - Christmas 2014

https://www.spoj.com/problems/XMAS2014/

Count ways to assign ≥d consecutive toys to a labeled box (Type 1: n < 2d,
at most one interval fits). Answer modulo m.

## Approach

For Type 1: choose a box (n ways) and an interval of length ≥d (there are
(n-d+1)(n-d+2)/2 such intervals). Answer = n*(n-d+1)*(n-d+2)/2 mod m.
Compute the product carefully to avoid overflow by halving the even factor.
