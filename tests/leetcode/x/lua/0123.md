Keep four running states while scanning the prices once: `buy1`, `sell1`, `buy2`, and `sell2`. `buy1` is the best balance after buying the first stock, `sell1` after selling the first stock, `buy2` after buying the second stock, and `sell2` after selling the second stock. For each price, update those states in order with the best value seen so far. This encodes all valid ways to use at most two transactions in `O(n)` time and `O(1)` extra space.
