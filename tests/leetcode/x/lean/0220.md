Keep a sliding window of at most `indexDiff` elements using buckets of width `valueDiff + 1`. Two numbers that differ by at most `valueDiff` must fall either in the same bucket or in neighboring buckets, so each new value only needs to check three buckets. After processing an index, remove the element that falls out of the window. This gives `O(n)` expected time and `O(indexDiff)` extra space.
