# Median Of Two Sorted Arrays

Merge the two sorted arrays with two pointers.

Walk through both arrays in order and append the smaller current value each time. Once one side is exhausted, append the rest of the other side. The merged array stays sorted, so the median is easy to read from the middle.

If the total length is odd, the median is the middle element. If it is even, the median is the average of the two middle elements.
