# Container With Most Water

Use two pointers.

Start one pointer at the left end and one at the right end. The current container area is the width between them times the shorter height.

After recording that area, move the pointer at the shorter line inward. Moving the taller line cannot help while the shorter line still limits the height, but moving the shorter line gives a chance to find a taller boundary with only a small width loss.

This checks all useful candidates in `O(n)` time.
