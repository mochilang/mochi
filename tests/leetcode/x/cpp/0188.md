If `k` is large enough to allow trading on every rise, the answer is just the sum of all positive day-to-day gains. Otherwise keep two arrays of length `k + 1`: `buy[t]` is the best balance after buying the `t`th stock, and `sell[t]` is the best balance after selling it. Scan prices once and update those states from `1` to `k`, which gives the best profit with at most `k` transactions in `O(nk)` time.
