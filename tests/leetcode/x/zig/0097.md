Let `dp[i][j]` mean whether the prefix `s3[0..i+j)` can be formed by interleaving `s1[0..i)` and `s2[0..j)`. The transition is straightforward: `dp[i][j]` is true if either the previous cell `dp[i-1][j]` is true and the next character comes from `s1`, or `dp[i][j-1]` is true and the next character comes from `s2`. The answer is `dp[len(s1)][len(s2)]`.
