If the total number of dresses is not divisible by the number of machines, balancing is impossible. Otherwise let `target = total / n` and scan from left to right. For each machine, compute `diff = machines[i] - target` and maintain the running prefix balance `flow += diff`, which tells us how many dresses must cross the boundary to the right. The minimum number of moves is the maximum over all positions of `abs(flow)` and `diff`, because a boundary may need a large net transfer and a single machine may need to send many dresses out on its own.
