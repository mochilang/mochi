Binary-search the answer instead of the subarray. For a candidate average `mid`, subtract `mid` from every element and ask whether there exists a subarray of length at least `k` whose transformed sum is non-negative. Prefix sums make that check linear: track the current transformed prefix, the minimum prefix seen far enough back to leave at least `k` elements, and accept `mid` once the difference becomes non-negative. Repeating that feasibility test for about 60 iterations gives stable double precision.
