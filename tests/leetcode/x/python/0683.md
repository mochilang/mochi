Convert the bloom order into a `days` array where `days[i]` is the day flower `i + 1` blooms. Then scan windows of length `k + 2`: if every interior flower blooms later than both ends, the two ends form a valid answer on the later of their bloom days. If an interior flower blooms too early, it destroys the current window and becomes the new left boundary. This yields a linear scan after building the days array.
