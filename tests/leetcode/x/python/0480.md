Maintain the lower half and upper half of the current window in two heaps, but remove expired elements lazily with a counter map instead of searching inside the heap. After each insertion and deletion, rebalance so the lower heap either matches the upper heap in size or has one extra element; the median is then either the top of the lower heap or the average of both tops. The implementation formats medians from doubled integers, which keeps outputs exact as integers or `.5` without floating-point noise.
