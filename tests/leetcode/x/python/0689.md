First compute the sum of every length-`k` window. Then build `left[i]`, the start index of the best window in `windows[0..i]`, preferring earlier indices on ties, and `right[i]`, the best window in `windows[i..]`, also preferring earlier indices by scanning from right to left with `>=`. Finally, sweep the middle window and combine `left[mid-k] + mid + right[mid+k]` to get the best total. This works because once the middle window is fixed, the best left and right windows are independent.
